Algonquin Tower

Location: Huntsville | Distance: 10km | Difficulty: Moderate

A challenging but rewarding climb through dense maple forests leading to an observation tower with panoramic views. The Algonquin Tower trail offers the highest elevation in the park, providing breathtaking 360-degree views of the canopy—perfect for photography enthusiasts.
